WINGs 2021: Winter retreat

On the 7th and 8th of January 2021, the first instalment of WINGs took place virtually on Zoom. The event gave participants the chance to present their work to an audience of women. Moreover, it featured talks from prominent mathematicians about their research and their careers: Prof Jessica Fintzen, Dr Susan Sierra, Dr Ana Rita Pires, and Prof Gwyneth Stallard.
